Welcome to the ADLINK-IST's Maven repository

To use the ADLINK-IST's Maven repository, add the following lines to your Maven settings or to your project's pom.xml file:

   <repositories>
      <repository>
         <id>adlink-ist-repo</id>
         <name>ADLINK-IST Maven repository</name>
         <url>http://adlink-ist.github.io/mvn-repo/releases</url>
      </repository>
   </repositories>
